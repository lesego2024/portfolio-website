THE FOUNDER'S JOURNEY

Driven by Experience, Defined by Design.

Lesego Incorporated was founded by **Lesego Matsoane**, a proud University of Johannesburg Graduate who achieved **3 Distinctions**. Our foundation is built on a rich background in corporate identity and digital innovation.

  • Interned at **Absa** during the 2018 rebranding from Barclays, gaining firsthand experience in large-scale corporate identity transitions.
  • Contributed graphic design work for the **Anthropology Department at UJ** while completing studies.
  • Served as a **UX/UI Engineer at Maptek/Minlog**, specializing in user experience and interface design for complex software.

Today, we leverage this expertise, **freelancing for the New Jerusalem Children's Home** (including designing their website: [jerusalemchildrenshome.org](http://jerusalemchildrenshome.org)) and providing graphic/package design for **Stationery Display Services** in Fordsburg.
